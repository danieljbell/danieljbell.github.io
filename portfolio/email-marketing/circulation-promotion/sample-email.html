In our 25 years of providing quarterly content through Bank Director magazine, we are excited to announce Bank Director is now a monthly publication!  We will continue to print our magazine each quarter, and in addition publish eight issues per year available only on your tablet or mobile devices via Bank Director's digital magazine app.

Unlike other digital magazines, which replicate print editions, each digital issue will deliver fresh, new content on issues facing the industry - content you will not find in our quarterly print edition.  The first digital edition focuses on legal and compliance issues in banking.  Future issues focus on topics such as attracting top talent, growing the bank, serving on the audit or risk committees, handling governance and technology in banking.

Designed to enhance your reading experience, the digital magazine app delivers interactive features including animated graphics, real-time polling, video interviews and social sharing.

About Bank Director

Since its inception in 1991, Bank Director has been a leading information resource for senior officers and directors of financial institutions. Chairmen, CEOs, CFOs, presidents and directors of banks and financial institutions turn to Bank Director to keep pace with the ever-changing landscape of the financial services industry.
